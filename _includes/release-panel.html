TVA is the authority for generation/release timing — get the schedule (or the TVA app) before every trip; it changes often. There is no USGS real-time flow gauge on the tailwater, so TVA's own schedule is the signal to trust. Sirens sound at the dam and Osceola Island but are inaudible well downstream.
