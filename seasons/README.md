# Seasons & Hatches

Seasonal timing and hatch notes — what's emerging when, and what to tie on. Build a chart per water (or a general one) over time; the pattern that matters is *local* timing, which only your own observations will nail.

## General hatch calendar (Northern Hemisphere trout — adjust to your water)

| Season | Typical activity | Flies to have ready |
|---|---|---|
| **Early spring** | Blue-Winged Olives (BWO), midges, early stoneflies | BWO #16–20, Griffith's Gnat, Pheasant Tail |
| **Late spring** | Hendricksons, March Browns, caddis start, big stoneflies (salmonfly/golden) | Caddis dries & pupae, mayfly duns, stimulators |
| **Summer** | Caddis, PMDs, tricos, terrestrials (hopper/ant/beetle), evening spinner falls | Hoppers, ants, Elk Hair Caddis, PMD patterns |
| **Fall** | BWOs return, October caddis, brown-trout streamer season, spawning aggression | BWOs, big streamers, egg patterns |
| **Winter** | Midges dominate, slow deep nymphing on tailwaters | Zebra Midge, small nymphs, tiny dries on warm afternoons |
