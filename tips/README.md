## Starter checklist — things worth writing down

- **Reading water** — where fish hold in runs, seams, pools, pocket water; the "feeding lane" concept.
- **Presentation** — drag-free drift, mending, reach cast, when to swing vs. dead-drift.
- **Casting** — loop control, distance, accuracy, casting in wind, roll cast, double haul.
- **Rigging** — leader/tippet formulas, dropper rigs, indicator depth, split-shot placement.
- **Knots** — clinch, improved clinch, surgeon's, blood, non-slip loop, nail knot. (A diagram or link per knot helps.)
- **Approach** — stealth, wading quietly, sun/shadow position, not lining fish.
- **Hooksets & landing** — timing by fly type, playing fish off the reel, netting, safe release.
