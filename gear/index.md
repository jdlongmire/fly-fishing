## Rules of thumb

- **Rod weight ↔ target:** small streams / trout on dries → 3–5 wt; general trout → 5–6 wt; bass/streamers/big water → 6–8 wt; salt → 8 wt+.
- **Leader ↔ fly:** heavier/wind-resistant flies need a stouter tippet to turn over; spooky fish on flat water need longer, finer.
- **Carry redundancy** for the cheap things that end a day: tippet, floatant, a spare leader, nippers.
