## Quick reference — pattern families

- **Dries** — float on the surface; imitate adult insects (Adams, Elk Hair Caddis, Parachute patterns).
- **Nymphs** — subsurface; imitate larval/pupal stages (Pheasant Tail, Hare's Ear, Copper John).
- **Emergers** — the vulnerable transition stage in/just under the film; often the killer when fish "won't take" dries.
- **Streamers** — imitate baitfish/leeches; stripped or swung (Woolly Bugger, Clouser).
- **Terrestrials** — land-borne insects that fall in (hoppers, ants, beetles); summer producers.
