# Catch Log

Dated trip entries. Copy [`_template.md`](_template.md) to a file named `YYYY-MM-DD-water.md` (e.g. `2026-04-12-south-holston.md`).

The point is accumulation: over a few seasons this becomes a searchable record of what produced, when, and under what conditions — the single most useful thing in the whole journal.
